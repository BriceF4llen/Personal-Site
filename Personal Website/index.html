About Me

Welcome to my personal website! Allow me the opportunity to introduce myself along with some of my work to you. I hope you enjoy the page and please feel free to get in touch!

Home

I'm fortunate enough to call two places home. I'm even more fortunate that they're both in Texas. Garland and Lubbock are my stomping grounds and I'm proud to come from them both. For all of it's faults, Texas is truly a very charming place. Y'all come visit now ya' hear?!

Family

I come from the most unlikely family imagineable considering how I turned out. I'm the product of an extended lineage of cotton farmers and ranchers on both sides. My dad is a small town banker and my mom works in the area where IT and Health Insurance collide.

Education

I've never been that great at learning in a formal classroom, but I graduated from Rowlett High School in 2011 and Texas Tech University in 2017. I am, however, very big on self teaching with Udemy. It's a great place to learn anything and it's also laughably affordable.

Projects

Below are 4 samples of past and current projects that I tinker with! I think these make a good sample selection for my work. Feel free to fork and contribute!

Waldo

Waldo was my Senior Capstone Project at university. It's a GPS prototype that utilizes an array of other modules on an Arduino microcontroller to log travel measurements into a .CSV file. The utilities can be endless, but I utilize it to walk my dog and record his speeds and map the path we took during the walk.
