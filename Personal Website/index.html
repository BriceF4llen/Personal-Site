About Me

Welcome to my personal website! Allow me the opportunity to introduce myself along with some of my work to you. I hope you enjoy the page and please feel free to get in touch!

Home

I'm fortunate enough to call two places home. I'm even more fortunate that they're both in Texas. Garland and Lubbock are my stomping grounds and I'm proud of my roots in both of these beautiful parts of Texas. For all it's faults, Texas is truly a very charming place. Y'all come visit now ya' hear?!

Family

I come from the most unlikely family imagineable considering who I've grown to be. I'm the product of an extended lineage of cotton farmers and ranchers on both sides. My dad is a small town banker and my mom works in health insurance. For me, family has and will always come first.

Education

I've always been a better self teacher than classroom learner, but I graduated from Rowlett High School in 2011 and Texas Tech University in 2017. I am a HUGE fan of Udemy and YouTube. They're great places to learn almost anything and they're also laughably affordable or free!

Projects

Waldo

Waldo was my Senior Capstone Project in college. It's a GPS prototype that utilizes an array of other instrument modules on an Arduino microcontroller to log travel measurements into a .CSV file. The utilities are endless, but I utilize it to walk my dog and record his speeds and map the path we took.
